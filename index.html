Lucid

A lightweight macOS accessibility utility designed to assist users with the physical cleaning of their Mac hardware.

It simply turns your screen into a pure black background with maximum brightness to help you spot smudges, while temporarily disabling the built-in keyboard and trackpad inputs to prevent accidental keystrokes while you wipe the hardware with a cloth.

Compliance Notice: Lucid is strictly a physical hardware accessibility tool. It does NOT clean disks, delete files, optimize system performance, or scan for malware. It is not a "system cleaner" or "PC tune-up" software.
