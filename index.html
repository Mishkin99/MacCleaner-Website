MacCleaner

A lightweight macOS utility software designed to assist users in physically cleaning their Mac computers. It temporarily disables the built-in keyboard and trackpad inputs to prevent accidental keystrokes or clicks during the cleaning process. It also turns the screen into a pure black background with maximum brightness to help users easily spot smudges and dust.
